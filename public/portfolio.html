About me

Hi, I'm Tiffany — a designer and engineer with a love for things that are both smart and beautiful. I'm currently studying Honours Systems Design Engineering at the University of Waterloo, where I think about how people interact with complex systems and how to make those interactions feel effortless.

My work lives at the intersection of UX design, front-end engineering, and creative problem-solving. I care about craft — the kind that shows up in the small details, the right typeface choice, the transition that feels just slightly better than expected. Good design is an act of respect for the person on the other side of the screen.

Outside of studio and lecture halls, you'll find me collecting fonts I'll probably never use, redesigning apps that don't need redesigning, and convincing myself that one more side project is a great idea. I'm always open to interesting collaborations, conversations, or a well-made matcha.
